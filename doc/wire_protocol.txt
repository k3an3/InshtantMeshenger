Inshtant Meshenger binary protocol (Version 1)

------------------------------
| 8 octets | (Length) octets |
------------------------------
| Preamble |     Body        |
------------------------------

Preamble Structure

----------------------------------------------
|  2 octets  | 1 octet | 3 octets | 2 octets |
----------------------------------------------
| Magic "IM" | Version | Reserved |  Length  |
----------------------------------------------

Body Structure

---------------------------------
| 1 octet | (Length - 1) octets |
---------------------------------
| Type ID | Type-specific data  |
---------------------------------

Message Structure

------------------------------------------------------
|    1 octet     | 16 octets  | (Length - 17) octets |
------------------------------------------------------
| Type ID (0x01) | Message ID |  Encrypted message   |
------------------------------------------------------
