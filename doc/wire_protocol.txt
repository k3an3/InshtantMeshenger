Inshtant Meshenger binary protocol (Version 2)

All multi-octet ints are in Network Byte Order (Big-endian)

------------------------------
| 24 octets | (Length) octets |
------------------------------
| Preamble  |     Body        |
------------------------------

Preamble Structure

--------------------------------------------------------------------
|  2 octets  | 1 octet | 2 octets | 1 octet | 2 octets | 16 octets |
--------------------------------------------------------------------
| Magic "IM" | Version | Reserved |  Type   |  Length  |    ID     |
--------------------------------------------------------------------

ClearMessage Structure (Type 0x01)

------------------------
|   (Length) octets    |
------------------------
|  Plaintext message   |
------------------------

Message Structure (Type 0x02)

---------------------------------------------------------------
|                    (Length) octets                          |
---------------------------------------------------------------
|                   Encrypted Message                         |
---------------------------------------------------------------
| 2 octets | 2 octets | (MLength octets)  | (SLength) octets  |
---------------------------------------------------------------
| MLength  | SLength  | Cleartext Message | Message Signature |
---------------------------------------------------------------

Pubkey Advert Structure (Type 0x03)

----------------------------------------
| 256 octets   | (Length - 256) octets |
----------------------------------------
| Name (ASCII) |      Public Key       |
----------------------------------------
