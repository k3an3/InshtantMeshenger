Inshtant Meshenger binary protocol (Version 1)

All multi-octet ints are in Network Byte Order (Big-endian)

------------------------------
| 8 octets | (Length) octets |
------------------------------
| Preamble |     Body        |
------------------------------

Preamble Structure

--------------------------------------------------------
|  2 octets  | 1 octet | 2 octets | 1 octet | 2 octets |
--------------------------------------------------------
| Magic "IM" | Version | Reserved |  Type   |  Length  |
--------------------------------------------------------

ClearMessage Structure (Type 0x01)

-------------------------------------
| 16 octets  | (Length - 16) octets |
-------------------------------------
| Message ID |  Plaintext message   |
-------------------------------------

Message Structure (Type 0x02)

-------------------------------------
| 16 octets  | (Length - 16) octets |
-------------------------------------
| Message ID |  Encrypted message   |
-------------------------------------
